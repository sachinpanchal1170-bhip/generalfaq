GENERAL FAQS
What types of printing products do you offer?

We offer banners, business cards, brochures, flyers, packaging, promotional products, signage, labels, and custom branding materials for businesses and events.

What is bleed and why is it important?

Bleed is the extra design area beyond the final trim size. It prevents unwanted white edges after cutting and ensures professional print quality.

What materials and finishes are available?

We provide matte, gloss, velvet lamination, textured papers, waterproof vinyl, eco-friendly stocks, UV coating, and premium card materials.

What is your standard turnaround time?

Standard turnaround time ranges from 2–7 business days depending on product type, order quantity, finishing options, and delivery location.

Do you provide custom size printing?

Yes. We offer fully customized print dimensions for banners, packaging, signage, stickers, and promotional products.

ACCOUNTS & ORDERS
Can I modify my order?

Yes. Orders can be modified before production starts. Once printing begins, changes may not be possible.

How can I track my order?

Tracking information is sent through email once your order has been shipped from our facility.

Can I cancel an order after payment?

Orders may be cancelled before production begins. Refund eligibility depends on the current stage of processing.

Do I need an account to place orders?

No. Guest checkout is available, but creating an account helps manage orders, invoices, and saved artwork files.

Can I reorder previous products?

Yes. Existing customers can reorder previous products using saved artwork and order history from their account dashboard.

PRODUCTS & MATERIALS
Are waterproof materials available?

Yes. Waterproof and weatherproof materials are available for outdoor banners, labels, signs, and promotional products.

Do you offer eco-friendly materials?

Yes. We provide recyclable papers, biodegradable packaging, and sustainable print solutions for environmentally conscious brands.

What paper thickness options are available?

We offer multiple GSM options ranging from lightweight flyers to premium thick business cards and packaging boards.

Are premium finishes available?

Yes. Premium finishes include spot UV, foil stamping, embossing, soft-touch lamination, and gloss coating.

Can I request product samples?

Yes. Sample kits are available for selected materials and finishes to help customers choose the best option.

PRINTING PROCESS
What printing methods do you use?

We provide digital, offset, UV, large-format, and screen printing services for various products.

Can I request print samples?

Yes. Sample proofs are available before final production begins for quality approval.

Do you provide color matching?

Yes. We support CMYK and Pantone color matching for accurate brand consistency.

Are double-sided prints available?

Yes. Double-sided printing is available for brochures, flyers, menus, and cards.

Can you print large-format banners?

Yes. We print large-format banners, posters, wall graphics, and exhibition displays.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY
How long does delivery take?

Standard shipping usually takes between 3–7 business days depending on location.

Do you offer express delivery?

Yes. Express shipping options are available during checkout for urgent orders.

Can I track my shipment?

Yes. Tracking details are sent through email after the order is dispatched.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. International shipping is available for selected countries worldwide.

What happens if delivery is delayed?

Our support team will provide updated delivery information and assist with tracking.

PRICING & PAYMENTS
Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept debit cards, credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes. Bulk quantity discounts are available depending on order size and products.

Can I request custom quotations?

Yes. Custom quotations are available for large or specialized print projects.

Are taxes included in pricing?

Taxes are calculated during checkout based on your billing and shipping region.

Do you provide corporate pricing?

Yes. Corporate and reseller pricing options are available for recurring business clients.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT
How can I contact support?

Support is available through phone, email, and live chat.

What are your support hours?

Our support team is available Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM.

Do you provide technical assistance?

Yes. We help customers with artwork setup, print files, and technical print issues.

Is live chat support available?

Yes. Live chat assistance is available during business hours for quick responses.

Can I request a callback?

Yes. Customers can request a callback through our support contact form.

RETURNS & REFUNDS
Can custom products be returned?

Returns are accepted only for damaged or incorrect items.

How long do refunds take?

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days.

What if my order arrives damaged?

Contact support within 48 hours with product images for replacement assistance.

Are shipping charges refundable?

Shipping charges are refundable only if the issue was caused by our production error.

Can I exchange printed products?

Exchanges are available for defective or incorrectly produced print items only.
