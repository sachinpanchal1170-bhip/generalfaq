GENERAL FAQS
What types of printing products do you offer?

We offer banners, brochures, packaging products, business cards, stickers, labels, and custom branding materials.

What file formats are supported?

We accept PDF, AI, PSD, PNG, EPS, SVG, and high-resolution JPG files.

Can I place bulk orders?

Yes. Bulk quantity printing services are available for businesses and agencies.

Do you provide design services?

Yes. Our design team helps with branding, layouts, and print-ready artwork.

What is your turnaround time?

Most standard orders are completed within 3–5 business days.

ACCOUNTS & ORDERS

Orders can be modified before production starts.

Tracking details are shared through email once the order is shipped.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled before printing begins.

Do I need an account to order?

No. Guest checkout is available for customers.

Yes. Previous order history is saved for registered customer accounts.

PRODUCTS & MATERIALS
Are waterproof materials available?

Yes. We provide waterproof and weather-resistant materials for outdoor banners, stickers, labels, signage, and promotional products.

What paper types do you offer?

We offer matte, gloss, silk, kraft, recycled, and premium cardstock paper options.

Can I choose custom finishes?

Yes. Matte, gloss, spot UV, foil stamping, and embossing finishes are available.

Are eco-friendly materials available?

Sustainable and recyclable material options are available for selected products.

Which materials are best for outdoor use?

Vinyl, acrylic, PVC, and UV-coated materials are ideal for outdoor use.
